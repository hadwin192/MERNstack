Quite

Artist: maoloow

Year of Creation: 2023

Description:

This isometric pixel art piece by maolow, captures a quiet, melancholic moment in a dense urban environment. A lone, dark-haired figure in a maroon sweater stands on a balcony, leaning against the railing. A warm yellow glow pours from the open doorway behind them, cutting through the dim, cool shadows of the night.
