The Star Pool

Artist: Stardustea

Year of Creation: 2026

Description:

Depiction of a fluffy rabbit drinking water. The rabbit rests on a mossy forest bank. A large wooden log sits behind the animal.The dark pond reflects a starry night sky. Tiny pixelated stars glimmer in the deep water. White cloud silhouettes frame the bottom reflection. The piece beautifully blends peaceful nature with cosmic details.
